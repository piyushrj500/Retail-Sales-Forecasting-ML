## 📌 Project Overview

This project develops an end-to-end machine learning solution to predict Walmart's weekly sales using historical retail data.

The complete workflow follows industry-standard data science practices including data understanding, exploratory data analysis (EDA), feature engineering, model development, model evaluation, and business reporting.

The objective is to help retailers forecast future sales and support better inventory management and business decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses experience fluctuations in weekly sales due to seasonal trends, holidays, weather conditions, and economic factors.

Accurate sales forecasting enables businesses to:

- Improve inventory planning
- Reduce stock shortages
- Optimize staffing
- Improve demand forecasting
- Increase profitability

# 💼 Business Insights

Major insights obtained from the analysis include:

- Significant variation in sales across stores.
- Holiday periods positively influence sales.
- Seasonal patterns affect weekly sales.
- Economic indicators show limited direct influence.
- Store-specific characteristics contribute significantly to prediction performance.

# 💡 Business Recommendations
